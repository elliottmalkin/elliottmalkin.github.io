Elliott Malkin
Product & UX
Elliott Malkin is a Staff Interaction Designer at Google Workspace. focusing on data analysis and ML-driven user assistance. Prior to Google, Elliott spent 4 years as Product Design Director for Mobile at The New York Times, building the company's first generation of mobile apps.
