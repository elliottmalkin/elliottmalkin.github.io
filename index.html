Elliott Malkin is a Staff Interaction Designer at Google Workspace currently focused on big data and ML-based user assistance.

Prior to Google, Elliott spent 4 years as Product Design Director at The New York Times, building the company's first generation of mobile apps.

Elliott was also an artist-in-residence at Eyebeam and has taught at Bard, Columbia, and NYU. In his spare time he likes to garden, teach coding, and read with his kids.
