Hyman’s Death Notice, 1960

A monument will be dedicated in loving memory of Hyman Victor, beloved father of Florence Lasky, Ethel Malkin, Valerie Alexander, Doris Kapp, and Ruth Gurvitz. Sunday, May 7, 1 p.m. Rezinoier. Roosevelt Road. gate 64, Waldheim.

Source: The Newberry Library, Chicago
