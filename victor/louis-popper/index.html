EXHIBIT 4 of 31

Louis Popper

Some time in 1913, Hyman set off alone, without his wife and five daughters, to meet his brother-in-law Louis Popper (Rose’s brother) in Chicago. Louis had already emigrated to Chicago from Rezhnoy. He is pictured on his gravestone in Chicago, just a few plots away from Rose and Hyman Victor.
